# Codebook - Tidy_Data - results from Getting and Cleaning Data Project - Sept 2015

The original data for the dataset can be found at: https://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/getdata%2Fprojectfiles%2FUCI%20HAR%20Dataset.zip 
and is detailed here: http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Human+Activity+Recognition+Using+Smartphones
